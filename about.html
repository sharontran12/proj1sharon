About

Recollection

Scrapbooking is a form of storytelling and a place to hold pieces of your memories combined with words or emojis to document your life in a meaningful way. Underneath a typical scrapbook that would usually include glitter, stickers, and decoration. For this particular scrapbook, I hope to focus on the true meaning of a scrapbook which would include simple designs.

For project 1, I created a virtual scrapbook that would emulate a physical scrapbook. While taking into consideration what a physical scrapbook would include, I added some personalized assets using HTML and CSS. As many people would flip a book, I used buttons as emulation for people to interact with the virtual scrapbook.

I used my personal images ranging from food, childhood, and growing into adult life pictures that led me to where I am. This form of a virtual scrapbook’s goal is to bring warmth and nostalgic memories. And it also gives the audience to get to know more about me and insights into my life.

As for the website design, I wanted to keep it minimalistic and simple using the main color brown as it is the color that represents soil and growth. The title of this scrapbook called “Recollection”. If I could sum up my virtual scrapbook into one word, I would title it “Recollection” since the title has a powerful meaning of recalling to mind or the reflections of memories.
